How It Works

1

Drop Your File

Select or drag & drop the file you want to share. Your file stays on your device.

2

Share the Link

Generate a unique sharing link or QR code and send it to your recipient.

3

Direct Transfer

When they open the link, a secure P2P connection is established for direct transfer.

Why PeerDrop?

End-to-End Encrypted

All transfers are secured with WebRTC's built-in encryption. Your data never passes through our servers.

No Size Limits

Share files of any size, limited only by your device's memory and connection speed.

No Waiting

No uploads to servers. No downloads from servers. Just direct device-to-device transfer.

Cross-Platform

Works on any modern browser. Share between mobile and desktop, Windows and Mac, iOS and Android.
