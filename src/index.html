How PeerDrop Works

PeerDrop uses WebRTC technology to create a secure, direct connection between devices. No data is stored on our servers.

1

Drop Your File

Select or drag & drop the file you want to share. Your file stays on your device until the recipient connects.

2

Share the Link

Generate a unique sharing link or QR code and send it to your recipient through any messaging app.

3

Direct Transfer

When they open the link, a secure P2P connection is established for direct device-to-device transfer.

Your data never leaves your device until it reaches your recipient
